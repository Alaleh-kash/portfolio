About me

I am a junior UX/UI designer in Canada. Fascination with art and design, from classical to modern, has always been a part of who I am. I love to mix design and technology to create things and solve problems through design. I care about the details and use modern and attractive designs. My goal is creating effective and intuitive interfaces.Even in my spare time, I love to find and solve problems,which explains my obsession with puzzles. With these in mind, I found UX/UI design as a skill that covers my interests and my abilities. I like prototyping, it helps me think through interaction models and new possibilities.I really love design. I have always been passionate about learning new skills and like working in a team. Painting, travelling, watching movies, cooking, and reading are all parts of my life thatallow me to be myself and enjoy life.
